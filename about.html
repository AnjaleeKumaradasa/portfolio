My Story

From my earliest school debates to professional keynote addresses, I've always believed in the transformative power of words. As a Sri Lankan communicator with international experience, I bring a unique multicultural perspective to every stage and page.

With a degree in Communications from the University of Colombo and over 8 years of professional speaking experience, I've had the privilege of addressing audiences across three continents. My approach blends rigorous preparation with spontaneous connection, ensuring every interaction feels both polished and personal.
